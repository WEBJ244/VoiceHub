Community guidelines — please read before submitting

Discrimination — Do not include language that demeans anyone based on race, gender, age, disability, sexual orientation, nationality, or any other protected characteristic.

Hate speech — Content that incites hostility, uses slurs, dehumanises individuals or groups, or is designed to intimidate will not be accepted.

Religion — Mocking or attacking any religion or its followers is not permitted. Legitimate concerns about religious accommodation in the workplace are welcome.

Politics — Party political campaigning or attributing workplace problems to someone's political beliefs is not permitted. Policy and process concerns remain welcome.

If your post is flagged — you will be prompted to edit and resubmit. Severe violations will be blocked entirely.
